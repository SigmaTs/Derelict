Privacy Policy - Derelict Bot

This Privacy Policy describes how Stay Enterprises ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Derelict Discord bot ("Bot").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Discord Data

We may collect the following information from Discord:

  • Server IDs: To identify servers where the Bot is used.
  • User IDs: To identify users interacting with the Bot.
  • Channel IDs: To identify channels where the Bot is used.
  • Message Content: If necessary for Bot functionality (e.g., commands, global chat).
  • User Roles: If necessary for Bot functionality (e.g., moderation).
  • User Nicknames/Usernames: For display purposes within the bot.

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect information about how users interact with the Bot, such as:

  • Command usage.
  • Interaction frequency.
  • Error logs.

1.3 Premium Data

If you purchase premium features, we may collect:

  • Transaction details (e.g., payment information, purchase history).
  • Premium feature usage.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bot's functionality.
  • Improve the Bot's performance and user experience.
  • Process premium feature purchases.
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Monitor and prevent abuse or misuse of the Bot.
  • Analyze usage trends and patterns.

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating the Bot (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors).
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law.
  • Third parties with your consent.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's functionality and comply with legal obligations.

6. User Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to the processing of your personal data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at stayenterprises@gmail.com or .shiguma. on Discord.
